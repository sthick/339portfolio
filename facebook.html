Facebook Ad Data

I was asked to pull the Facebook Ad data for all Cavalier related accounts and add them to our Looker Dashboards and Amazon S3 buckets for future analysis. This required communicating with team members in order to get the correct access credentials for the Facebook accounts as well as learning and implementing the facebook API. Once the data was pulled it was added to the Looker connections where I cleaned the data and used it to make visualizationf for the marketing department.
